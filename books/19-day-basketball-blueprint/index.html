About This Book

19 Days. Real Results.

Most basketball improvement guides give you a laundry list of drills with no structure. You end up doing random exercises with no clear progression, wondering why your game isn't getting better. The 19 Day Basketball Blueprint takes a different approach.

This is a structured, day-by-day plan that builds on itself. Each day focuses on specific fundamentals — shooting, passing, dribbling, and game IQ — with clear progressions that compound over the 19-day period. By the end, you'll have built real, lasting habits that translate directly to game performance.

Whether you're a player looking to level up before tryouts, a parent training with your kid, or a coach designing an offseason program — this blueprint gives you the roadmap.

What You'll Learn

  • A complete 19-day structured training program
  • Progressive shooting drills — form, rhythm, and game shots
  • Ball handling sequences that build coordination and confidence
  • Passing drills that develop court vision and decision-making
  • Game IQ exercises to read defenses and make better decisions
  • How to maintain and build on your improvements after day 19
