About This Book

The Dribble Drive System

Basketball is trending toward positionless play. The days of rigid lineups with a traditional 1-through-5 are fading. If you want your team to play the way the modern game is evolving, the Dribble Drive Motion Offense is one of the best systems you can install.

Built on one core principle — attack the gaps — the Dribble Drive makes every player on the floor a potential driver. Every drive creates a reaction from the defense that opens up a teammate. It's aggressive, it's unpredictable, and it's devastating when executed properly.

This book takes you from the basic concepts to advanced reads, with drills to install each piece, quick hitters for structured possessions, and secondary break options to score in transition.

What You'll Learn

  • Core principles: attack gaps, kick, and fill
  • Basic and advanced player reads and decision trees
  • Positionless lineup strategies — the Warriors model
  • Installation drills that build the system piece by piece
  • Quick hitters for structured looks within the motion
  • Secondary break options for transition scoring
