The Journey

From the Court to the Page

Coach DeForest's coaching career spans over two decades of work at every level of the game. From youth development leagues to elite high school programs, every season brought new lessons — and a deeper understanding of what it really takes to build winners.

After years of coaches asking him to share his systems, drills, and philosophies, Coach DeForest decided to put pen to paper. The result? A growing library of basketball ebooks that distill the best of his experience into practical, ready-to-use content.

His books aren't academic — they're direct, opinionated, and built for coaches who want to get better now. Every chapter is a tool. Every page is a competitive advantage.
