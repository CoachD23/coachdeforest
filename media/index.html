Speaker Bio

Coach Lee DeForest

Lee DeForest has coached basketball for almost 20 years, winning a state championship in 2011 with his variation of the Princeton Offense. A published author of five books on basketball strategy, his work on offensive systems has connected him with coaches around the world.

Growing up in a small town in Kentucky with big dreams, he learned to love the game spending time in the gym with his father and learned to love writing after graduating from law school. His books have earned over 160 Amazon reviews with a 4.5-star average rating.
