The High-Low Chapter

Bill Self's Multiple Option Offense at Kansas looks complicated on tape. It isn't. It's a series of options off the same initial action — and the high-low entry is the one that's launched dozens of pros and a national championship.

The sample chapter walks through the entry, the read, and the counter — what happens when the post defender plays in front, when he plays behind, and when the defense tries to switch. Three reads. That's the whole chapter.

In the Sample

  • The three primary reads and how to coach the decision
  • What to do when the defense fronts the post
  • How Coach Self counters a switch (and why most teams don't)
  • Two drills to install the high-low in a single practice
