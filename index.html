The Author

Coach Lee DeForest

A basketball coach for almost 20 years, Lee DeForest learned to love the game spending time in the gym with his father and learned to love writing after graduating from law school. Most noted for his variation of the Princeton Offense, he won a state title in 2011.

Growing up in a small town in Kentucky with big dreams, he's published books on the Princeton Basketball Offense that have helped him make friends all over the world.
